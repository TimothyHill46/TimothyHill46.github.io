What Are MOBA Game?

Timothy Hill May 11, 2025

What Are MOBA Games?

If you already know what MOBA games are, you can skip this whole post.

Game FunctionÂ 

Multiplayer Online Battle Arena! In this game format, players are split between 2 teams in multiple lanes, commonly 3. The goal in these MOBA games is to "kill the keep." You have 5 players on each team with unlimited lives. However, if you die, you need to wait until you revive. As the game goes on, the timer gets longer and longer. Each game is limited to its own session; the last game session will not be transferred to the next game session.

Shops

You also have a shop there to get items and intern get more stats. These stats can make or break your game so you best get items that suit character.

"But how would you pay for these items" you might ask; well by killing the enemy's troops/players/buildings, and you also obtain gold passively.

Items and their stats vary from game to game, deadlock has items that can modify your weapon's reload whereas League of Legends will not have such items.

What is the goal of these games?

You need to move into the enemy's base and destroy their keep, however, in most of these games, you will have to deal with enemy creatures known as minions, towers, and some form of buffing building!

The minions are simple creatures that are mainly there for you to earn gold once you kill them, they do a small amount of damage, but be careful, if you aggro too many of them then even a hero can't defend against them.

The towers are more of an equalizer able to destroy the minions quickly, but the tower does not share the gold with you in most cases. The tower does serve as a protector, if you attack an enemy hero under their tower then you will be targeted, so be mindful of that. You have to kill each tower within that lane to enter into the enemy's base.

In the enemy's base, you have a buffing building that, once destroyed, can buff your ally minions. The buffs differ greatly per game but they are very helpful for your team! These buildings generally have no protection except for other players or minions.

What makes MOBA games fun?

These games tend to be fast-paced and requires a lot of teamwork and skill. People find these games enjoyable because of the competitiveness of the game, and the fact that the game is limitless. There is no endgame with each match not transferring over to the next. The main appeal of this game is the number of characters you can play, the number of builds you can go with each character, and the fact that you will play with different people the next time you play again.

Who are these games not for?

Solo players, and people who want to see progress. These games are amazing time wasters and provide you with little to no grand stats or achievements. If you are someone wanting to see a skill, a number, a quest, or anything go up, MOBA games are not for you. If are you wanting a semi-quick game then this is it!

What MOBA games are out there?

Traditional MOBA games like League of Legends is a popular one, DOTA 2 is another good one, then you have some non traditional MOBA games like Deadlock and SMITE. a full list will be provided and maintained when reviews for these games are made.
